    Climateberkeley weather year round
    Thanks to its home on the San Francisco bay Berkeley enjoys a mild climate that is refreshing year round. In the summer you will see highs in the 70's and lows in the 50s. In the winter you can expect highs in the low 60s and lows in 40s. On average the warmest month is September and the coolest is January.
